About Us

Hello beautiful people! Welcome to The Matcha Bar, a
paradise for matcha people.

Here, we guarantee a place full of calmness and trueness
as our matcha-based beverages are made passionately to promote enthusiasm that you can taste on your first sip.

Excited about it? Then be with us on your Matcha
Journey!

Mission


To offer peace and love while enjoying the real taste of
matcha. We dedicate every bit of our flavor to feeling the
connection among our customers while having an
unforgettable experience through our beverages.


Vision


Become the go-to spot for matcha lovers with
exceptional customer service. We, The Matcha Bar foster
wellness, creativity, and serenity.
